    As a Certified Airmax Repair Center, Legacy Waters is authorized to perform warranty-covered repairs on Airmax equipment like PondSeries aerators and LakeSeries fountains, which can reduce downtime and out-of-pocket costs.   • How does aeration support long-term results after dredging or vegetation harvesting?

    After hydraulic dredging removes built-up sediment, properly sized Airmax aeration systems help maintain depth and clarity by slowing future muck accumulation. Continuous circulation and oxygenation also stabilize water quality, making it easier to manage algae, weeds, and nutrients over time so your restoration investment lasts longer.

  • Is Airmax aeration right for Mid-Atlantic ponds and lakes like mine?

    Yes, Legacy Waters designs Airmax aeration plans specifically for Mid-Atlantic conditions, from community stormwater ponds to larger private lakes across Maryland and the surrounding region.   • What problems can Airmax aeration help prevent in my waterway?

    Proper aeration reduces stagnant zones where algae blooms, foul odors, and excessive muck are likely to form. It also helps limit invasive aquatic weeds, supports stronger fish populations, and can make it easier to stay in compliance with local water quality and stormwater regulations.
