Common Questions

Airmax Aeration Questions? We Have Answers.

  • What is an Airmax aeration system and how does it improve my pond or lake?

    An Airmax aeration system uses bottom-diffused air to increase dissolved oxygen across the entire water column, which helps reduce muck, algae, and foul odors while supporting healthier fish and aquatic life. By preventing stagnation and improving circulation, Airmax systems also enhance water clarity and help protect the results of dredging and vegetation removal over the long term.

  • Why should I choose a Certified Airmax Repair Center instead of a general pond service?

    A Certified Airmax Repair Center is trained and authorized by Airmax Eco to install, maintain, troubleshoot, and repair Airmax compressors, control panels, diffusers, and fountains to factory standards. This means you can access warranty-covered repairs, faster diagnosis, and properly sized equipment, which reduces downtime, avoids costly mistakes, and extends the life of your aeration system.

  • How does Legacy Waters design an Airmax aeration plan for my specific property?

    Legacy Waters starts with a complimentary pond or lake profile that considers surface area, depth, shoreline shape, existing issues like algae or muck, and your goals for aesthetics and fish habitat. Using Airmax tools and training, the team then recommends the right PondSeries or LakeSeries aeration kits, fountains, and controls, and can integrate them with dredging, harvesting, and ongoing management for Mid‑Atlantic conditions.

  • What types of Airmax services do you offer (installation, repair, and maintenance)?

    As a Certified Airmax Repair Center, Legacy Waters provides full-lifecycle service, including new system design and installation, seasonal start‑ups and winterization, proactive maintenance, and warranty and out‑of‑warranty repairs on Airmax aeration systems and fountains. Technicians are trained through the Airmax Eco eCAST program to properly service compressors, diffusers, control panels, and electrical components so your system runs efficiently year‑round.

  • How quickly can Airmax aeration improve water quality after dredging or vegetation removal?

    In many ponds and lakes, properly sized Airmax aeration begins improving circulation and dissolved oxygen within days, which helps stabilize conditions after dredging or vegetation harvesting. Visible changes in clarity, odor, and muck reduction can take several weeks to a full season, depending on nutrient loads, depth, and how long the waterbody has been stagnant, but aeration is critical to protecting your restoration investment.

  • Do you only serve Maryland, or can you help with Airmax projects across the Mid‑Atlantic?

    Legacy Waters is based in Bel Air, Maryland, and regularly serves ponds and lakes throughout the Mid‑Atlantic, offering regional expertise in local regulations, climate, and watershed conditions. If you manage multiple properties or larger lakes outside central Maryland, the team can evaluate your site and either provide direct service or coordinate a scalable Airmax plan tailored to your portfolio.
